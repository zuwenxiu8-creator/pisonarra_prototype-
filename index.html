Data Privacy & Security Agreement

Thank you for choosing PisoNarra. To provide a secure and optimized lending experience, we collect specific account and device information only for registration, verification, risk control and service operation.

Phone Number Information: used to establish your secure account, verify identity, and send essential service communications.

SMS Information: optional access may be requested strictly to detect OTPs during login and secure transactions. We do not read, store, or monitor personal conversations.

Location Permissions: used to verify service availability in your region and detect abnormal login locations.

Device Information: used for fraud prevention, troubleshooting and account security.

Camera Permissions: used for ID upload, OCR, selfie capture and face verification.

Installed Application List: if requested, it is used only for basic security diagnostics and fraud prevention.

By tapping Agree, you acknowledge that permissions can be managed through your device settings. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
